Mr. T. Carlos Kerwillain

Software Engineer, Meta (Facebook)




Mr. T. Carlos Kerwillain, a dedicated Software Engineer and devoted father of two children,
has crafted a path of academic and professional success through perseverance and passion for technology. Born and raised in Liberia,
Mr. Kerwillain completed his secondary education at Revelation SDA High School, graduating in 2022/2023. During this time, his curiosity
for computers and programming began to flourish, setting the stage for his future career. In pursuit of higher education,Carlos
embarked on an academic journey that led him to Kigali Independent University in Rwanda.

There, he diligently pursued his Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering, culminating in his graduation in 2026/2027.
His time in Rwanda not only honed his technical skills but also broadened his cultural horizons, preparing him for a global career in technology.
Following his undergraduate studies, Mr. Kerwillain was driven to deepen his expertise in the field of information technology. This drive led him
to Harvard University in the United States, where he pursued and successfully obtained a Master's Degree in Management of Internet Systems (MIS),
at Harvard, he engaged with cutting-edge research and industry leaders, further refining his knowledge and strategic insights into the evolving
digital landscape.

Professionally, Mr. T. Carlos Kerwillain has excelled in various roles within the software engineering domain, leveraging his expertise to solve
complex technical challenges and contribute meaningfully to innovative projects. His commitment to excellence and continuous learning has earned
him recognition among peers and leaders in the industry. Outside of his professional endeavors, Carlos finds immense joy and fulfillment in his
roles as a husband to Tarpee Thomas and a father to his two children. His family serves as a source of inspiration and motivation, grounding him in
values of love, responsibility, and balance. Looking ahead, Mr. Kerwillain remains dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology, contributing to
impactful projects, and nurturing a loving and supportive home environment for his family.
